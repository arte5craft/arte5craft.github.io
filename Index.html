01 — FEB 2026
GaitWay

A wearable capstan-drive mechanism designed to assist leg lifting for stroke rehabilitation. Engineered to deliver ~48 Nm of torque under tight motor, space, and $350 budget constraints. Dyneema rope was chosen to minimise creep and ensure stable, repeatable actuation. The compact, body-mounted system successfully assisted human gait at natural walking pace.

🏆 Breakthrough Innovation Award
Capstan Drive Wearable Rehabilitation Mechatronics
